Sports

I grew up playing sports for as long as I can remember. I have two very profund memories from prior to 2010 that I remember growing up was when I was 3 years old playing Tee ball and playing Mini hockey with my dad in the living room. When I became old enough I played as many sports as possible and that continued all the way up to the last year of high school. I played volleyball, basketball, soccer, baseball, you name it i played. I also wanted to be on every sports team possible because it mean't a free day of playing sports and nothing sounds more appealing to me than 'sports all day'. My main sport was hockey then in my grade 9 year of high school I played Lacrosse for the first time and it was history since. I fell in love with the game and now here I am playing at the University of Ottawa for the Mens team. The mens lacrosse team has a pretty big instagram following. Their Instagram As a team we like to use the social media to promote team events or charity games that we do. For example this past weekend we played a game against Queens University. The game was not just a regular game, it was a game for Beau. A minor lacrosse player who was coming home from a tournament got into a car accident with his father. His father did not comeout of the crash and beau was in a coma for a month or two. We posted the link on our instagram and made it clear all the funds donated at the game would be going straight to Beau and his family. Donations for Battle for Beau. Overall, If I had to choose my 3 favourite sports, I think my top 3 would be:

  • Lacrosse
  • Hockey
  • Basketball
